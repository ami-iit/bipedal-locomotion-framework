Template parameters
index a positive number from 0 to 2.

Note

0 is associated to the variablesHandler, 1 to the weights and 2 to the TSID. Thanks to this method the TaskSpaceInverseDynamics behaves as a tuple. I.e., the std::tie() or Structured binding declaration are supported by TaskSpaceInverseDynamics.
