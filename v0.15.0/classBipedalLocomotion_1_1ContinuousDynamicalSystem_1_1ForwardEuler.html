Warning

We assume that the operator + is defined for the objects contained in the DynamicalSystem::State and DynamicalSystem::StateDerivative.

The ForwardEuler integrator is compatible with Lie groups defined by the manif library. Since the box-plus operator is not commutative for a Lie group, here we consider only manifold left plus

where belongs to a Lie group and belongs to the tangent space.
