Warning

We assume that the operator + is defined for the objects contained in the DynamicalSystem::State and DynamicalSystem::StateDerivative.

The RK4 integrator is compatible only with tuple containing vectors belonging to the space. It is not compatible with Lie groups. If you want to integrate a dynamical system defined on a Lie group please use the ForwardEuler integrator.
